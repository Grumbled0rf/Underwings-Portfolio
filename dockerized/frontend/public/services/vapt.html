Our Approach

VAPT Methodology

We follow industry-standard methodologies (OWASP, PTES, NIST) combined with real-world attack techniques.

1. Scoping & Reconnaissance

Define scope, gather intelligence, map attack surface, identify technologies and potential entry points.

2. Vulnerability Assessment

Automated scanning combined with manual analysis to identify security weaknesses and misconfigurations.

3. Exploitation & Validation

Safe exploitation of vulnerabilities to validate risk, demonstrate impact, and chain findings.

4. Reporting & Remediation

Detailed technical report with risk ratings, proof-of-concept, and prioritized remediation guidance.
